# Who We Serve

Ubunifu SACCO serves every kind of builder in Uganda's tech ecosystem:

- **Software Developers:** Freelance, employed, or founder-track. Device finance, cloud credits, income-smoothing savings.
- **Startup Founders:** Pre-seed to Series A. Revenue-share loans, milestone disbursement, co-founder-friendly terms.
- **Digital Creatives:** Designers, videographers, animators. Equipment leasing, invoice advance, portfolio fund.
- **Women in Tech:** Lower rates, peer-guarantee model, mentorship pairing through Women in Technology Uganda.
- **Student Innovators:** Makerere, Mbarara, Kyambogo. Zero-balance savings, prototype grants, pre-graduation membership.
- **Research Commercializers:** Academic innovators going lab-to-market. IP-aware loans that never touch your core work.
